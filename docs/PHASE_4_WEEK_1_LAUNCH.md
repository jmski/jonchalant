# Phase 4 Full Launch - Week 1 Action Plan

## This Week (March 4-10): Foundation Setup

### Monday-Tuesday: Email & Lead Magnet Foundation

#### Task 1: Choose Email Platform
**Pick one:**
- **Mailchimp** (free up to 500 contacts, easiest start)
- **ConvertKit** (best for creators, $25/month)
- **Substack** (free newsletter platform)

**Action**: Sign up, create account, brand it

#### Task 2: Create First Lead Magnet
**Create: "Executive Presence Audit Tool" (PDF)**
- Simple self-assessment (10 questions)
- Results scoring (1-5 scale)
- 5 persona descriptions (Invisible → Commanding)
- Personalized recommendations
- Next steps suggestion
- Design: Simple 2-3 page PDF (Canva template or Google Docs)

**Why**: Quick win, high value, drives email signups

**Time**: 1-2 hours

#### Task 3: Build Email Welcome Sequence
**Create 5 emails (write the copy):**
1. Welcome email + audit PDF delivery
2. "Why You're Already a Good Leader" (reframe)
3. "The One Thing Holding You Back" (problem ID)
4. "How Quiet Command Works" (framework intro)
5. "Your Next Step" (CTA to audit or coaching)

**Note**: Don't code/upload yet, just write the copy in a Google Doc

**Time**: 2-3 hours
