## Common Questions & Answers

### Q: Isn't this just executive coaching?

**A:** Not exactly. Executive coaching often assumes you need to become louder or more extroverted. I teach the opposite: how to lead authentically as yourself. I blend body language psychology (from movement), communication mechanics (from sales), and introvert psychology to build sustainable presence.

### Q: Will this change who I am?

**A:** No. One of my core philosophies is "No Performance Required." The goal isn't to become someone else—it's to become fully, authentically yourself and let that presence speak. You'll be more confident in your own skin, not pretending to be someone you're not.

### Q: How is this different from public speaking training?

**A:** Public speaking classes often focus on slides and scripts. I focus on YOU—your body language, your energy, how people perceive you. The speaking skills follow naturally once you master presence. Plus, presence matters in one-on-ones, meetings, and interviews—not just on stage.

### Q: Why "Kinetic"?

**A:** Kinetic means "motion" or "movement." It represents the idea that presence comes from your body first—how you move, how you stand, how you occupy space. It's active, dynamic, powerful. The opposite of frozen or uncertain.

## Authenticity Check

Before writing or posting anything under The Kinetic Leader brand, ask:

1. **Does it come from personal experience?** (Not borrowed from other coaches)
2. **Would I say this to a friend struggling with this?** (Natural, not "salesy")
3. **Does it honor the introvert experience?** (Respectful, not dismissive)
4. **Is it about transformation, not criticism?** (Building up, not tearing down)
5. **Could an introvert say "yes, that's me"?** (Relatable, specific)
