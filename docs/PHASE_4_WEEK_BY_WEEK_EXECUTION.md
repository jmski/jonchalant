- Ongoing passive lead generation (email + SEO)

## Key Success Principles

1. **Batch similar work**: Write all outlines at once, record all videos in one session
2. **Repurpose content**: One blog post → 1 video + multiple social posts + 1 email
3. **Consistency over perfection**: Published is better than perfect; you can always improve
4. **Monitor + adjust**: Check analytics weekly, notice patterns, double down on what works
5. **Engage your audience**: Reply to all comments, replies, DMs (builds community)
6. **Think long-term**: This 8-week sprint creates 6-12 months of evergreen content + authority
