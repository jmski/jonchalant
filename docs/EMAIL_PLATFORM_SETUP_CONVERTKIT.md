# Email Platform Setup - Recommendation & Implementation Guide

## Platform Recommendation: ConvertKit

**Why ConvertKit?**

- **Best for creators**: Built specifically for content creators (not just marketing)
- **Beautiful integrations**: Links perfectly with your blog, landing pages, products
- **Creator-first pricing**: $29/month (no overage fees)
- **Automation**: Seamless sequences, broadcast emails, subscriber segments
- **Deliverability**: High inbox placement rates (important for coaches)
- **Growth features**: Built-in landing pages, embeds, referral links
- **Community**: Strong creator community + resources

**Alternative considerations:**

- **Mailchimp**: Free up to 500 contacts, but clunky interface for creators
- **Substack**: Great for newsletters, but less e-commerce/coaching friendly
- **ActiveCampaign**: Powerful but more expensive ($99+/month) and complex

**Decision: ConvertKit is the sweet spot for you.**

## Phase 1: Account Setup (Day 1-2)

### Step 1: Create Your ConvertKit Account

1. Go to convertkit.com
2. Sign up (email + password)
3. Name: "Jonchalant" or "Jon [Last Name]"
4. Brand: Leadership coaching for introverts

### Step 2: Complete Your Brand Profile

1. **Profile picture**: Use your professional headshot (or professional photo)
2. **Bio**: "I help introverts build executive presence and quiet command. 8-12 week coaching programs + free lessons."
3. **Website URL**: jonchalant.com
4. **Social links**: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube

### Step 3: Create Your Email Sign-Up Form

1. Go to "Subscribers" → "Signup forms"
2. Create a form called: "Free Executive Presence Lessons"
3. Form fields:
   - First name (required)
   - Email (required)
4. Form message: "Join 5,000+ leaders building executive presence. Get one powerful lesson per week + exclusive resources."
5. Design: Keep it simple, match your brand colors
6. Redirect after signup: "Thanks for joining! Check your email for your first lesson."

### Step 4: Set Up Your Welcome Sequence

1. Go to "Automations" → "+ New automation"
2. Name it: "Welcome Sequence - Executive Presence"
3. Trigger: "When someone subscribes to my form"
4. Add the 5 emails (copy from the EMAIL_WELCOME_SEQUENCE document)
5. Set timing:
   - Email 1: Immediate (upon signup)
   - Email 2: +2 days
   - Email 3: +4 days
   - Email 4: +6 days
   - Email 5: +9 days
6. After final email: Unsubscribe from automation, move to "Weekly Lessons" list

### Step 5: Create Your "Weekly Lessons" List

1. Go to "Subscribers" → "Subscriber lists"
2. Create new list: "Weekly Lessons - Executive Presence"
3. This is where people move after completing the welcome sequence
4. You'll send lessons to this list 1x per week

### Step 6: Embed Your Signup Form on Website

1. In ConvertKit, go to your signup form
2. Copy the embed code
3. Add to your website footer (all pages) or create a dedicated signup page
4. Test it works

## Phase 2: Lead Magnet Setup (Day 2-3)

### Create Your Lead Magnet Landing Page in ConvertKit

1. Go to "Landing pages" → "+ New landing page"
2. Page name: "Executive Presence Audit"
3. Page layout: Simple, clean
4. Content sections:
   - **Headline**: "Your Executive Presence Audit" + subtitle
   - **Description**: "Discover where you stand on the presence spectrum—and what to focus on first."
   - **Form**: "Free Executive Presence Lessons" form (the one you created above)
   - **Button text**: "Get my free audit"

### Upload Your Lead Magnet PDF

1. Create the PDF version of the audit (from LEAD_MAGNET document)
   - Use Canva (fast, templates) or Google Docs > Export as PDF
   - Keep it clean, 2-3 pages, branded
2. In ConvertKit:
   - Go to your automation (Welcome Sequence)
   - In Email 1, add a link to your PDF
   - Host the PDF in ConvertKit's file manager OR send as an email attachment
3. Set Email 1 to include:
   - Welcome message
   - "Download your audit" link
   - Direct link to PDF (or attachment)

### Create URL for Your Lead Magnet

ConvertKit will give you a unique landing page URL:
`convertkit.com/jonchalant-executive-presence-audit` (or similar)

Use this URL:

- In your website footer
- In social media bios
- In your blog posts (CTAs)
- Everywhere you want to drive signups

## Phase 4: Tracking & Optimization (Week 1+)

### Dashboard Metrics to Monitor

1. **Signup form metrics**:
   - Views (how many people see your form)
   - Signups (conversions)
   - Conversion rate (goal: 20-30%)

2. **Email metrics**:
   - Open rate (goal: 40%+)
   - Click rate (goal: 20%+)
   - Unsubscribe rate (normal: 0-2%)
   - Reply rate (goal: 5-10%)

3. **Automation performance**:
   - How many people complete each email
   - Where people drop off
   - Reply messages (read these!)

### Week 1 Actions:

- [ ] Monitor welcome sequence opens
- [ ] Read all replies to emails
- [ ] Note common questions (incorporate into future emails)
- [ ] Check form conversion rate

### Week 2 Actions:

- [ ] A/B test subject lines
- [ ] Optimize form CTAs based on performance
- [ ] Plan your first "Weekly Lesson" email

### Create Your "Weekly Lesson" Email Template

1. Go to "Broadcasts" → "+ New broadcast"
2. Create a recurring email (weekly, Tuesdays at 9 AM)
3. Template structure:

---

**Email Template: Weekly Lesson Format**

**Subject**: [Question or promise about the pillar/topic]

**Preview**: One line teaser

**Body**:

- Hook (1-2 lines)
- Main lesson (300-500 words)
- Practical insight or exercise
- Call-to-action
- Signature

**CTA Options**:

- Link to free lessons page
- Link to book a coaching call
- Link to group program
- Link to blog post
