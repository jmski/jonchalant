**First 3 Videos to Record**:

#### Video 1: "What IS Executive Presence?" (5 min)

- **Outline**:
  - Hook: "Most people get this wrong..." (attention grab)
  - Definition: Clear, introvert-friendly explanation
  - 3 Key Components: Physical, Verbal, Mental
  - Why it matters: Career impact data
  - CTA: "Subscribe for more lessons"
  - Outro: Channel quality teaser
- **Talking Points**:
  - Executive presence ≠ charisma or extroversion
  - It's about influence and authority
  - Introverts are naturally suited for this

#### Video 2: "The 3 Pillars of Quiet Command" (6 min)

- **Outline**:
  - Intro: "Here's my framework..."
  - Pillar 1: Physical Presence (posture, stance, energy)
  - Pillar 2: Verbal Presence (voice, clarity, intentionality)
  - Pillar 3: Mental Presence (conviction, calm)
  - How They Work Together: Example scenario
  - Why This Works for Introverts: Your unique advantage
  - CTA: Watch the full blog post / join email list
- **Talking Points**: Real-world examples
- **B-roll**: Could include clips of you standing/speaking

#### Video 3: "Why Introverts Make Better Leaders" (5 min)

- **Outline**:
  - Hook: Counter the narrative ("Everyone says introverts are bad leaders...")
  - Myth vs Reality: Data + research
  - 5 Introvert Superpowers: Listening, depth, strategic thinking, etc.
  - Your Perspective: Why you believe this
  - Real Examples: Companies/leaders (Satya Nadella, Bill Gates, etc.)
  - CTA: Start your free audit
- **Visuals**: Keep it simple; focus on your presence and energy

**[ ] Recording Setup**:

- **Equipment**: Phone (good camera) or webcam + simple background
- **Lighting**: Natural window light OR ring light ($20-40)
- **Audio**: Phone mic is fine; speak clearly
- **Background**: Clean, professional (bookshelf, branded wall, neutral)
- **Editing**: Free tool CapCut (mobile or desktop)
- **Upload**: Unlisted first (for review), then publish

**Publishing Cadence**:

- Video 1: Week 4, Monday
- Video 2: Week 4, Friday
- Video 3: Week 5, Wednesday
- Then: 2-3 videos per week (sustainable pace)
