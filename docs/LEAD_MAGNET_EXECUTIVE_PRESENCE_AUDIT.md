### Page 2-3: Results & Personas

**Transfer your section scores to find your Executive Presence Profile:**

---

#### PROFILE 1: The Invisible Leader (Total Score: 10-18)

**You**: Struggle with presence in high-stakes moments. People often miss your contributions because they don't see or hear you.

**Your Strengths**: Thoughtful, reflective, good listener, strategic thinker

**Your Challenge**: Being seen and heard; confidence in visibility

**Your Next Step**: Start with Physical Grounding. Master your body language first. It's the fastest way to shift how others perceive you. (Estimated: 2-4 weeks)

---

#### PROFILE 2: The Quiet Contributor (Total Score: 19-26)

**You**: You have moments of presence, but it's inconsistent. Sometimes you show up powerfully; other times you shrink.

**Your Strengths**: Self-aware, willing to grow, capable of stepping up

**Your Challenge**: Consistency and confidence in unfamiliar settings

**Your Next Step**: Start with Vocal Presence. Learn to speak with clarity and intention in any room. This builds momentum quickly. (Estimated: 4-6 weeks)

---

#### PROFILE 3: The Steady Presence (Total Score: 27-34)

**You**: You've got a solid foundation. People respect you and take you seriously most of the time.

**Your Strengths**: Authentic, reliable, influencers, consistent

**Your Challenge**: Breaking through to executive-level impact; fine-tuning presence

**Your Next Step**: Focus on Strategic Communication. Elevate your impact by communicating with greater intentionality and influence. (Estimated: 6-8 weeks)

---

#### PROFILE 4: The Emerging Command (Total Score: 35-42)

**You**: You're already a strong presence. People listen when you speak, and you command respect.

**Your Strengths**: Confident, clear communicator, self-assured, commanding

**Your Challenge**: Deepening authenticity and presence with vulnerability; advanced influence

**Your Next Step**: Advanced Executive Confidence. Integrate all 5 pillars for complete, sustainable presence. (Estimated: 8-12 weeks)

---

#### PROFILE 5: The Commanding Presence (Total Score: 43-50)

**You**: You're already there. You have executive presence in nearly every situation—physical, vocal, confident, strategic, authentic.

**Your Strengths**: Natural leader, commanding, influential, people follow your lead

**Your Challenge**: Sustaining it; helping others develop it

**Your Next Step**: Consider how you want to leverage this. Are you ready to teach others? (Leadership coaching, workshops, mentoring?)
